A look inside

Capture in a tap, find by asking, and a quiet record of where each note happened — in light or dark.

Built for the way ideas actually arrive

Messy, mid-sentence, at the worst possible moment. Musing catches them and gives them back when you need them.

✶

Capture anything, instantly

Type, talk, or snap a photo. Voice notes keep the actual audio, not just the transcript — and the cleanup keeps your voice, so a half-formed line stays a half-formed line.

⌕

Never organize again

No folders, no tags, no sorting. Every note quietly remembers its context at capture — place, time, weather — so you find things later by simply asking.

✷

Find it by asking

“What was that café idea when it was raining?” Ask in plain language and Claude finds the notes that match — ranked by relevance, even when you don’t recall the exact words.

❖

Reads your photos

Attach an image and Claude reads the text and names what’s in it — a plant, a landmark, a dish — folded right into the note, and fully searchable.

⚡

Capture from anywhere

A Home Screen widget, three Siri phrases, the Shortcuts app, and the share sheet — so a thought is never more than a tap or a word away.

⛨

Private by design

Notes are stored on your device and backed up to your private iCloud. AI runs through Anthropic’s Claude. Nothing is ever sold, and nothing is used to train models.
